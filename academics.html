MAKING IT COUNT

Although they are looked upon as little fingers but as the time ticks growth sets in and the future is here. That is the reason their actions and inactions at this their time of know nothing is to be apportioned the precious attention that it deserves.

At Al Aflaz we inculcate into our kids the essential knowledge required to live a fruitful life without regrets. They are precious and are treated as such. This is the foundation and its strength is not only determined by the amount of time put into it but also by the quality of undaunted efforts put into the time.

As a goal, we are aimed at producing future leaders who are bold, diligent, intelligent, humble, charismatic, dogged and stand not only for justice but will be just themselves. These are achieved through well-detailed knowledge of Islam and the authentic sayings of the prophet.

We are poised at giving the best academic standard that they deserve, through the use of our detailed and professionally designed curriculum. Our goal is to lay a promising foundation for the kids and prepare them to be one of the best amongst their contemporaries. Our standard is to produce kids that would have memorised a large portion(more than half if not all) of the Qur’an upon passing out and would be able to read fluently from the mushaf. Speak basic Arabic, memorise some hadiths and also know the adhkar required to run their day to day activities, thus equipping them spiritually. We also build them with sound morals and characters. Ample time is invested in their sporting activities, art and crafts. We build their public speaking capabilities with our weekly public speaking program. There is the termly spelling bee where we play around with words. The strength of their hifz (Qur’an memorization) is tested by our monthly musabaqah.
